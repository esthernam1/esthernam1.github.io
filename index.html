CONCEPT.

For this project, I created a speculative prototype of a packaged food item. I wanted to focus on several design aspects in terms of user experience, aesthetics, and structure. Using Unity and Adobe Illustrator to configure augmented reality on the structure, I wanted people to be able to interact and connect with the food they will be eating through a form of technology. Users will be able to use their phone cameras to scan the image target and see the 3D models of what specific foods are best for your bones, muscles, or teeth. This project developed from my love of food and nutritional facts.  Although the product does not promise any scientifically proven result, interacting with new things and feeling better about what you’re eating is an advantage within itself.
