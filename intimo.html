Creating community with user generated audio of people during quarantine.

We are making an online radio experience event that employs user-generated sound bites to help people connect. Private listening on headphones is a way to experience intimacy during mandated social distancing. Our social interface gives users a way to share snippets of audio(with loved ones) while socially <-- 6 ft --> distant.
