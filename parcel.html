Parcel Post
Sending & receiving parcels with seamless interactions

Parcel Post provides users in big cities to deliver documents, food, devices, people, and other special packages to people local to the city. After interviewing several users who often send mail, I wanted to design a new experience of delivering packages locally and without having to leave your house with just a few clicks.

The aim of this app is to send and receive parcels faster and more efficiently, simplify the process, and make it more accessible by standardizing the prices.
