Tiny Planet
Building an interactive planet controlled by the palms of your hands

Tiny Planet is a collaborative project created in Unity using the Leap Motion sensor and our team's recorded motion capture data. As a team, we wanted to explore how light, space, and scale can affect us, as well as individually, in the phenomenology of our own design practice. We were heavily inspired by the story of Jack and the Beanstalk and Gulliver's Travel to create a dystopian planet than can be controlled by a giant(user).

For the final, we were able to create two different iterations of what we imagined our scene to be.
